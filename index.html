About HelixDrop

Welcome to HelixDrop!

HelixDrop is a thrilling, challenging, and addictive vertical ball-drop game inspired by the popular Helix Jump. Experience the excitement of navigating a bouncing ball through rotating helix platforms in this HTML5 arcade masterpiece.

Developed with passion for casual gamers of all ages, HelixDrop delivers fast-paced, mobile-friendly gaming without requiring downloads or registration. Jump in and start playing instantly!

Game Features

  • 3D-style visual effects
  • Progressive difficulty levels
  • Smooth physics simulation
  • Touch and keyboard controls
  • Mobile-optimized gameplay
  • Local high score tracking

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

HelixDrop is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best gaming experience:

  • Game Data: Your scores and game progress are stored locally on your device using browser local storage.
  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage data to improve our game performance and user experience.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or process any personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details.

Cookies and Local Storage

Our website uses:

  • Local Storage: To save your game progress, high scores, and preferences locally on your device.

Data Security

Since we don't collect personal information, there's minimal risk to your privacy. All game data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers. We implement standard web security practices to protect our website and ensure a safe gaming environment.

Children's Privacy
